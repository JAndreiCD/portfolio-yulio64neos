This is a portfolio-ready concept for small businesses that need a professional website without unnecessary complexity.

The goal is to create a fast, responsive, and easy-to-navigate website that presents services, builds trust, and helps potential customers get in touch.

The project focuses on clear content structure, mobile-first design, performance, accessibility, and a clean contact flow.
